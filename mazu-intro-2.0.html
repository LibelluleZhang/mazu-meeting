缘起Origin

她站在崖边，只是在乎She stood at the cliff's edge, and simply cared

北宋年间，福建莆田湄洲岛，一个叫林默的渔村少女开始在崖边望海。她不是英雄，不呼风唤雨。她只是站在那里，为出海的人守着那条回家的海路。人们说，被她接住的人，说不清怎么得救的，只说「有人在看着」。 In the Northern Song dynasty, on Meizhou Island off the coast of Fujian, a young woman named Lin Mo began watching the sea from the cliffs. She was no hero — she didn't command the waves. She simply stood there, keeping watch so fishermen could find their way home. Those she saved couldn't explain how — they only said, "Someone was watching."

一千年后，林默变成了妈祖——全球数亿人信仰的海上守护神。但剥离所有神性和香火，那个最初的她只是一个「在乎」的人。这份在乎，是中国人骨子里的东西：不需要轰轰烈烈，只是在别人需要的时候，站在你能站到的地方。 A thousand years later, Lin Mo became Mazu — the sea goddess worshipped by hundreds of millions worldwide. But stripped of divinity and incense, she was simply a person who cared. This caring is something deeply Chinese: it doesn't require grand gestures — just being where you're needed, when you're needed.

每个人都做过被接住的人。你生命里，一定也有一个"妈祖时刻"——不需要拯救世界，只是在将要跌倒的时候，有一双手托了你一下。 We've all been held at some point. You have a "Mazu moment" too — not a world-saving miracle, just a hand that caught you when you were about to fall. 这不是关于神的故事。这是关于「被接住」的故事。This isn't a story about a goddess. It's about being held.

备案名称「妈祖」已通过国家广电。妈祖是唯一被允许展现神迹的中国神明——4亿信众的基础流量，不需要从零建认知。核心目标像《黑神话：悟空》那样：让不了解妈祖文化的人，看完第一集就想看第二集。 Registered title "Mazu" cleared by China's NRTA. Mazu is the only Chinese deity permitted to display miracles on screen — 400-million-strong existing belief base means zero cold-start. Goal: like Black Myth: Wukong — make people who know nothing about Mazu culture finish episode one and need episode two.

真

真Truth

不动妈祖本体形象。通过现代视角、配角互动侧面切入。拒绝平铺直叙的传记式流水账——用故事吸引人，不是用说教。 Don't depict Mazu directly. Approach through modern perspectives and supporting characters. No biographical chronology — attract with story, not preaching.

善

善Goodness

聚焦疗愈——不是「有求必应的神」，而是帮人应对迷茫、获得面对风险的勇气。类似现代心理支持的文化载体。 Focus on healing — not a wish-granting deity, but a cultural vessel that helps people face uncertainty and find courage. Like modern psychological support in ancient form.

美

美Beauty

国风视觉诗，AI 与人的共创。画面要有呼吸感，像宋画可以走进去。突出海洋文明「包容不确定性」vs 农耕文明保守——呼应「人生海海」。 Guofeng visual poetry, co-created by AI and humans. Breathing compositions you can walk into. Highlight maritime culture's embrace of uncertainty vs agrarian conservatism — echoing "life is like the sea."

三重主题：女性主义（智慧女性的解决问题能力）+ 两岸联结（信仰共同性）+ 海外认同（掷圣杯解身份焦虑）。不仅是古代传说，更是当代人身份认同与地方联结的纽带。 Three themes: Feminism (wise women solving problems) + Cross-strait connection (shared belief) + Overseas identity (casting moon blocks to resolve belonging anxiety). Not just ancient legend — a thread connecting contemporary identity and place.

不是随便丢 idea——每一套都已经有了具体的叙事结构和情绪落点。展开看细节。 Not random ideas — each has a concrete narrative structure and emotional anchor. Expand to see details.

聚焦林默从人到神的顿悟时刻——她遇难前的思考、死后的迷茫、最终的选择。核心不是她有多厉害，而是「她为什么选择成神」。Focus on Lin Mo's moment of awakening — her thoughts before the tragedy, her disorientation after death, her ultimate choice. The core: why did she choose to become divine?

适合快节奏短剧，开场即震撼。每集一个侧面的顿悟，拼出完整的弧光。Perfect for fast-paced shorts. Each episode reveals one facet of awakening, building the complete arc.

一个有文化认同困境的海外华裔青年——中国人不认为他是中国人，美国人不认为他是美国人。经历漫长的寻根之旅后，来到妈祖庙前，掷出圣杯，问：「我到底是中国人还是美国人？」An overseas Chinese youth caught between identities — Chinese don't see him as Chinese, Americans don't see him as American. After a long journey seeking roots, he kneels before Mazu's temple, casts moon blocks, and asks: "Am I Chinese or American?"

giulianoooooo 补充：圣杯是容错机制。掷出圣杯，代表神明同意你做这件事。它给人面对不确定性的勇气。Director adds: Moon blocks are a fault-tolerance mechanism. Casting a sacred cup means the deity consents. It gives people courage to face uncertainty.

每集一个现代困境——可能是一个对女性主义感到迷茫的女孩，可能是一个什么都不信的男生——穿越或在梦中与林默相遇。林默用她的方式解决问题，现代角色从她身上获得启发。核心是对比与顿悟。Each episode: a modern dilemma — a girl confused about feminism, a guy who believes in nothing — encounters Lin Mo through time or dreams. Lin Mo solves problems her way; the modern character finds inspiration. Core: contrast and awakening.

不局限于任何一个民族或地区。妈祖概念可以关联二进制、数字化、现代科技——科学和神学的边界在今天已经模糊。格局放到全球，可能某几集根本没有妈祖，但一看就知道在讲妈祖。Not bound to any single ethnicity or region. Mazu's concept can connect to binary code, digitalization, modern tech — the boundary between science and theology is blurred today. Global scope: some episodes may never name Mazu, but you'll know it's about her.

叙事结构Narrative Structure

妈祖人间分身串联
每3–5集一个独立故事A mortal avatar of Mazu anchors the series
Every 3–5 episodes = one standalone story

不是流水账也不是单元剧——妈祖以人间分身贯穿全季，像一条线串起珍珠。每3–5集构成一个完整故事单元，可以独立观看，合在一起又是一条成长的弧光。 Not a chronology, not disconnected shorts — Mazu's mortal avatar threads through the entire season, like a string through pearls. Every 3–5 episodes form a complete story unit, watchable standalone, yet together they trace an arc of growth.

01

分身串联Avatar Thread

妈祖以人间分身贯穿24集——她可能是不同时代的不同身份，但始终在做同一件事：站在需要的人身边。分身是锚点，独立故事是浪。Mazu appears through a mortal avatar across 24 episodes — different eras, different identities, but always the same mission: standing beside those in need. The avatar is the anchor; standalone stories are the waves.

02

3–5集故事单元3–5 Episode Story Blocks

每3–5集构成一个独立故事。快节奏，剧情密度高——看完一个单元就是一个完整的「被接住」体验。参考短剧的爽感节奏，但用电影级审美来拍。Every 3–5 episodes form one complete story. Fast-paced, dense narrative — finish a block and you've experienced a full "being held" journey. Short-drama pacing, cinematic aesthetic.

03

二创赛道Fan Creation Track

后续开放——不是做完就结束。世界观和 IP 一旦立住，开放二创：同人故事、平行宇宙、角色前传。让妈祖从一个剧变成一个「可以参与的世界」。Post-launch phase: once the world and IP are established, open fan creation — side stories, parallel universes, character prequels. Transform Mazu from a series into a "world you can participate in."
