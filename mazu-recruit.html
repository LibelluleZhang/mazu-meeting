我们在做什么What We're Building

113|113|113|

首部AI国风诗选短剧。叫妈祖，但镜头始终对着每一个具体的人。The first AI-powered Guofeng anthology series. It's called Mazu, but every frame is about ordinary people.

114|114|114|

妈祖最动人的地方不是她能飞天遁海，是她记得所有人的名字。一个渔村少女，16岁开始站在崖边望海。不是因为有人要求她，是因为她在乎。What makes Mazu extraordinary isn't that she could command the seas — it's that she remembered everyone's name. A girl from a fishing village, standing at the cliff's edge at sixteen. Not because anyone told her to. Because she cared.

115|115|115|

这种在乎就是我们要拍的东西。不是神迹，不是法力，是一个人看见另一个人。That caring is what we want to capture. Not miracles. Not divine power. Just one person seeing another.

这些方向让我们兴奋What excites us

132|132|132|
人物是具体的。情感是安静的。改变是看不见的。Characters are specific. Emotions are quiet. Change is invisible.
133|133|133|
妈祖作为精神符号，出发的勇气，归航的慰藉。不直接出场。Mazu as a spiritual anchor — courage to depart, comfort in return. Never appears directly.
134|134|134|
海洋文明：包容不确定性，鼓励冒险突破。Maritime civilization: embracing uncertainty, encouraging boldness.
135|135|135|
活态信仰：泉州浔埔村，福州天后宫。鲜活的人的信仰。Living faith: Xunpu Village in Quanzhou, Tianhou Temple in Fuzhou. Belief as people actually live it.

我们避开这些What we avoid

138|138|138|
妈祖显灵救了所有人。神迹里观众找不到自己。Mazu performs a miracle and saves everyone. Spectacle leaves no room for the viewer.
139|139|139|
「我学到了妈祖的伟大」。说教。"I learned the greatness of Mazu." Moralizing.
140|140|140|
主角顿悟、痛哭、跪拜。廉价。真正的改变是安静的。Epiphanies, weeping, kneeling. Cheap. Real change is quiet.
141|141|141|
景区化标准庙宇叙事。Sanitized, tourist-brochure temple narratives.
